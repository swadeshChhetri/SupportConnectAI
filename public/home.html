FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about how SupportConnectAI fits into your workflows? Here are answers to common questions.

How does the AI learn?
SupportConnectAI learns by ingesting documents you upload (like PDFs, DOCX, TXT), scraping public documentation or FAQ URLs, or direct manual entries. Our specialized embedding model processes these resources in real-time to build a custom semantic knowledge base specific to your company guidelines and brand voice.
What document types are supported?
We support a wide variety of documentation formats including PDF manuals, Help Center articles, FAQs, policy sheets, text files, and Markdown. You can also paste direct website links to automatically crawl and import pages.
Can I customize the widget?
Absolutely. You can customize the widget theme colors, bot avatar, header titles, welcome messages, placeholder text, and initial prompt buttons to align perfectly with your brand style guidelines.
What happens when AI cannot answer?
When the AI encounters a question outside its knowledge base or if the confidence score drops below your set threshold, it instantly prompts the customer to create a support ticket. The system captures the full chat transcript and automatically notifies your team in the Agent Workspace.
How are tickets assigned?
Tickets are automatically generated in the Agent Workspace with full AI-summarized context. They can be auto-assigned to support reps based on custom routing rules, department tags (e.g., Billing, Technical), or claimed manually by agents in the live queue.
Is organization data isolated?
Yes, security is a core pillar. SupportConnectAI is built on a strict multi-tenant architecture. All uploaded files, crawled URLs, and chat logs are compartmentalized within organization-specific sandbox databases. Your business data is never shared across organizations or used for training public models.
